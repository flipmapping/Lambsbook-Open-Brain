TITLE:
Governance Reasoning Engine Becomes the Next Root Capability

STATUS:
active

DATE:
2026-06-23

DECISION:

The next major architectural milestone for the Open Brain is the Governance Reasoning Engine.

The Open Brain shall evolve beyond knowledge storage and retrieval into an active governance and decision-support system.

Rather than merely storing authority artifacts, the system will reason over certified truths, relationships, decisions, and lessons to guide execution.

RATIONALE:

The Open Brain has established the foundational capabilities required for semantic reasoning:

✓ Truth Capture
✓ Lesson Capture
✓ Decision Capture
✓ Relationship Capture
✓ Authority Index
✓ Relationship Index
✓ Authority Search
✓ Graph Traversal
✓ Impact Analysis
✓ Execution Governance Doctrine

These capabilities provide sufficient semantic structure to begin reasoning over knowledge rather than only retrieving it.

OBJECTIVES:

The Governance Reasoning Engine shall answer questions such as:

• Which governance doctrines apply to this request?
• Which execution phase is active?
• What certified evidence is still missing?
• Is mutation currently authorized?
• Which authority artifacts justify this recommendation?
• What downstream impact will this mutation have?

LONG-TERM VISION:

Knowledge Storage
        ↓
Knowledge Navigation
        ↓
Knowledge Reasoning
        ↓
Governance Decision Support
        ↓
Autonomous Execution Guidance

EXPECTED OUTCOME:

Future execution recommendations become evidence-driven, graph-aware, and traceable to certified authority artifacts rather than relying on keyword search or historical memory alone.
