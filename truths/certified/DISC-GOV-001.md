EVIDENCE:
Multiple convergence investigations revealed that mutations performed before obtaining authoritative evidence frequently resulted in rework, incorrect assumptions, runtime instability, and governance drift.

The project adopted a recurring execution doctrine requiring inspection before mutation.

This doctrine was repeatedly applied during:
- Runtime investigations
- Supabase investigations
- MVP convergence
- Open Brain development
- Governance validation

TRUTH:
No mutation should occur before authoritative truth has been retrieved and inspected.

MEANING:
Evidence retrieval precedes action.

Assumptions are not sufficient grounds for mutation.

RULE:
Inspect
    ↓
Verify
    ↓
Understand
    ↓
Mutate

Never reverse the order.
